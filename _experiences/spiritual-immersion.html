SPIRITUAL IMMERSION

A visit to a Balinese healer can be a truly transcendental experience that goes far beyond simple physical healing and is rooted in age-old traditions, allowing you to reconnect with yourself and find balance and harmony. According to Balinese belief, the human body is crossed by energy channels called “nadis”. When these channels are blocked or unbalanced, physical, emotional, or spiritual health issues can arise. The healer uses a variety of techniques to rebalance energies and to restore harmony in these channels. Additionally, this holistic approach can help soothe physical pain, improve sleep, and awaken spiritual awareness.
