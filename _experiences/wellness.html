Spiritual Rituals and Chakra Balancing

The journey continues with a delicious Megibung-style Balinese meal. This unique culinary tradition invites you for a convivial gastronomic experience where you eat with your hands, served on a large banana leaf, symbolizing sharing and unity. Taste exquisite flavours and discover the richness of Balinese cuisine. Then, create your natural massage oil in the company of a Balinese priest, with a some local spices and cooking over a wood fire! The end point of this day is a 45-minute massage serenaded by the sounds of the jungle, where you can indulge in the use of the freshly created massage oil!

Step into the mystical realm of Balinese spirituality with a bespoke purification ceremony set amidst verdant greenery and sacred waters, designed to cleanse body, mind, and spirit. Guided by the temple guardian, immerse yourself in the transformative energy of water, paving the way for a profound inner journey. Following the ritual, delve into a private chakra reading session, where our expert will harmonize your energy centers for enhanced well-being. Then, venture to the home of the Balinese high priest's family for a Megibung-style lunch, where cultural treasures await, including traditional Gender music, century-old Wayang Kulit puppets, and insights into Balinese daily life. Conclude your day with a blessing and purification ceremony at the family temple, leaving you enriched by the authentic spiritual and cultural experiences of Bali.
