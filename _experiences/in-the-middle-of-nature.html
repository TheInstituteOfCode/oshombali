IN THE MIDDLE OF NATURE

Discover the natural wonders of Bali and visit an honey farm with unique breed of bees that don’t sting. You will taste the purest honey straight from the honeycomb, and take home a jar of this golden goodness (or several) as a souvenir! Afterwards, hop on a scooter with your Balinese driver for a short ride through the natural landscapes at 20km/h to arrive to a natural vanilla farm. The farmer will explain and show you the fascinating and long process of cultivating this precious spice. Also take the opportunity to taste freshly picked fruit from his garden! You will leave with a sachet of vanilla (containing at least 6 pods) as a souvenir.
