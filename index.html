ABOUT NUANU

\_

Nuanu is a creative city spanning 44 hectares in Bali, Indonesia, that embodies a commitment to harmonious living. A vibrant community of creators, leaders, and changemakers are empowered here to foster a culture of positive change. Designed as an integrated ecosystem, the city features dedicated spaces for education, art & culture, wellness, entertainment, and nature-inspired living, envisioning a future where these elements seamlessly intertwine.

THE EARTH SENTINELS

The Earth sentinels represent some long forgotten ancient ancestors of ours, who like the oldest trees have been petrified in stone and retaken by the forest (plants and greenery weaving with the sculptures). They are guardians of the pathway into the events area that will remind us everyday of the importance of our relationship and the sanctity of our natural world; the forests, mountains and wild lands.

img_path: /uploads/earth-sentinel.jpeg img_alt: pic 1 text-alignment: left url: - col-content-html: >-

THE LIGHTHOUSE

The lighthouse is called the Tower of Bhuma, which means the ‘Earth’ in Sanskrit, and it’s a symbol of the Earth being protected by three petals and showing the fragility of our earth by Arthur Mamou-Mani. The Tower of Bhuma is a symbol of hope and sustainability.
